About Me

Hello! I am a rising senior at the University of San Diego pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. This summer I will be a web application development intern with ConvergeOne in Minneapolis Minnesota, where I am originally from.

In the summer of 2023 an internship with OnCorps Reports introduced me to web development, where I worked on an ASP.NET Webforms application. Working on a web application showed me a way to apply my interests in visual arts developing meaningful services and efficient user experiences. So began my interest in programming to build things that you can interact with directly, specifically web applications. In university, I learned more of the theoretical knowledge for the web development concepts that I was putting in practice. My appreciation for object-oriented programming grew. As a highly organized person, the concepts of object-oriented programming (Abstraction, Polymorphism, Inheritance, Encapsulation) just make sense to me and give me opportunities to shine. With object-oriented code, I can make connections. I believe that making connections between concepts and code and everything in between is the best way to 1) learn, 2) solve problems, and 3) understand the "why" behind things.

In my time off from school I've learned a lot more about modern ASP.NET Core Web APIs and MVC applications. But aside from coding, I love to listen to music, draw, journal, lift weights, go for walks, and spend time with friends and family.

Feel free to check out my LinkedIn profile or my art account on Instagram.
